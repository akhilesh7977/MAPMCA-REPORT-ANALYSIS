# 📊 Fruits Arrival Trends & Rate Analysis (MAPMC)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes **fruit arrival trends and price variations** in the MAPMC market using interactive dashboards.  
It helps in understanding seasonal demand, pricing patterns, and market insights for better decision-making.

---

## 🚀 Key Insights

- 📦 **Total Market Volume**: Handled over **987K+ tonnes** of fruit arrivals, indicating high market activity.
- 📈 **Peak Arrival Year**: Significant surge observed in **2024–2025**, showing increased supply or demand.
- 💰 **Price Volatility**: Rates ranged from **₹61 to ₹92/kg**, highlighting notable price fluctuations.
- 📊 **Stable Average Pricing**: Average price remained around **₹77/kg**, suggesting moderate market stability.
- 🥭 **Top Contributing Fruits**: Few fruits (like Apple, Mango, Watermelon) dominate overall market share.
- 📅 **Seasonal Trends**: Clear seasonal spikes in arrivals, especially during peak harvest months.
- 📉 **Supply vs Price Relationship**: Higher arrivals often led to **price drops**, indicating demand-supply impact.
- 🔍 **Year-wise Growth Pattern**: Gradual growth trend with sudden spikes, useful for forecasting.
- 📊 **Market Concentration**: Majority of arrivals are concentrated in limited fruit categories.
- ⚡ **Decision Insight**: Data can help traders optimize **buy/sell timing** for maximum profit.
